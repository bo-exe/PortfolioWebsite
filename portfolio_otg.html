Design Process Details

Brief Summary:

This app was created as a way for me to have an easily accessible portfolio. Thus the name, Portfolio On-The-Go.

Included in this app are projects that I have done in the past, a section about me, and also a section for my audience to contact me via my socials etc.

The main tagline of my app was "Merging Creativity with Technology.".

I found this tagline extremely relevant and impactful. As a student taking a diploma in Digital design and Development, we make use of our creativity and technological knowledge a lot.

I went for a slightly more minimal and nature-focused design for this app.

Key Demographic:

The app's main target audience will be UX/UI designers and companies looking for inspiration or talent in the UI/UX sector who can bring fresh perspectives to their team.

The app uses minimalistic and nature-focused theme which is the trend for most designs and companies.

Being in a industry heavily focused on technology, companies and individuals will be interested in staying updated with the latest trends, tools, and technologies.

As a Singaporean, there will also be a few companies that admire and seek local talent as that can help to show the unique cultural influences of our region.
