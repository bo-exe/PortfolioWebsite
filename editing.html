Design Process Details

Brief Summary:

During my holidays, I worked with a Profession E-Sports athelete who competed in one of the biggest global stages in one of the biggest games in the world.

The game is called VALORANT and the biggest tournament she participated in was the VALORANT Champions Tour 2022: Game Changers Championship.

I was given the opportunity to edit youtube videos for her as she wanted to push out som content on her youtube channel in order to grow her audience and reach.

During this time, I would take raw footages of streams that she would have live on her channel twitch.tv/sonder and cut them down into long form videos of around 8 minutes to 10 minutes.

The most popular video of hers that was edited by me was a video titled "The WORST game of VALORANT I have ever played...".

The video reached 50,000 views in the first 12 days of upload.

As of the time of writing this, the video is sitting at 68,269 views.

Everything from reviewing footage, cutting out and merging parts of the videos, to the thumbnails and descriptions were all done by me using opinions, feedbacks, and my own creativity.

This was one of my proudest projects ever as I felt like during this time, I have learnt a lot about video editing and I was also extremely proud of how the videos came out.
