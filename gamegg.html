Design Process Details

Brief Summary:

Game.GG is a news and shopping website for people who game or are interested in games.

Game.GG curates the latest gaming news and updates, providing a centralised platform for users to access all the information they need to stay ahead in the gaming world.

Game.GG provides news from tournament updates to game releases and patches to ensure that gamers all around the world are kept updated.

The website also offers a vibrant and engaged gaming community where users can make use of to connect with other gamers, participate in discussions, and share their experiences and insights.

Key Demographic:

The target audience of this website will be gamers or users that are interested in the gaming industry.

The website provides up-to-date news and informations which gamers can rely on to stay updated on the things that are happening in or to their favourite games.

Not only that, the website also provides a way for the community to connect and make friends which is a useful tool for people looking for groups or people to play games or enjoy their hobbies with.

The website also allows users to purchase things such as powdered energy drinks to ensure they have the energy to keep grinding!
