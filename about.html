About Me.

I'm Koh Bo Yang, a student from Republic Polytechnic, currently taking a Diploma in Digital Design and Development.

Since young, I have always indulged myself in creativity-driven activities such as learning instruments, drawing, and editing. Though I may not be the best at these fields, I always find joy in them as they allow me to express my creativity in different ways.

I have also always been a big fan of technology, often doing research and learning more. At the age of 15, I even built my own personal computer. Since then, my love for technology has been ever-growing.

During my free time, I like to game, watch shows, or listen to music.

A reason why I took this diploma was because I hoped and believed that this course will not only allow me to express my creativity but also allow me to learn more about the IT side of things.
