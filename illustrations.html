Castle on the ice

Design Process Details

Brief Summary:

This project was one of the first few projects I did on photoshop and it was created as a way for me to learn and practice few functions of Adobe Photoshop.

The process for doing this project was quite simple.

First, I took an image of a castle and another separate image of iceberg so I can crop and overlay them.

I started off by simply choosing an area where I want to overlay the iceberg over and place the castle in.

After that, I cropped out a part of the iceberg in order to make space for the castle and layer the cropped out iceberg over the castle.

Then, I duplicated the castle layer, mirrored it, added a filter and lowered the opacity in order to make the reflection of the castle on the water.

I also used the paint tool to add shadows around certain edges of the iceberg and castle to make it look more realistic.

Tools used:

Adobe - Photoshop

Fighting Covid-19

Design Process Details

Brief Summary:

Just like the previous project, this project was one of the first few projects I did on illustrator and it was created as a way for me to learn and practice few functions of Adobe Illustrator.

The main idea of this piece was to create a piece of art to inspire and educate the audience about Covid-19 prevention.

Before I started working on the art of this project, I took some time to brainstorm on what I wanted the final work to look like.

Since it was about fighting Covid-19, I was inspired by the concept of a game 'Street Fighter'.

With that idea in mind, I designed a health bar inspired by the game and created a sanitizer bottle character to fight against the Covid-19 Virus.

As for the background, I took inspiration from the shophouses and streets of Singapore.

Tools used:

Adobe - Illustrator

GAME.GG Logo

Design Process Details

Brief Summary:

The idea of this project was to create a logo for a website I was developing called Game.GG.

The website is a site that allows gamers and those interested in gaming related news to stay up-to-date

To create the logo, I went to Adobe illustrator to create a globe as I felt like a globe would best represent the global reach the site has.

The globe is also a way to represent that news provided on the site can cater to people all over the world.

Then, I took the globe and went to photoshop and added the texts GAME GG around the globe.

To add on, I created a gradient from bright red to neon green as I felt that those color give off the Futuristic feels to audiences.

The futuristic colors helps to emphasize the point that Game.GG stays up-to-date.

Tools used:

Adobe - Photoshop

Brewed With Love

Design Process Details

Brief Summary:

For this project, I had planned to design a name card for a coffe and tea brewing company I had created called "Brewed With Love".

I created the card following a nature theme by using shades of green and by adding branches of leaves on the corners of the card.

I also added the tagline "The Best For You." At the bottom of the card in the front together with the logo I created for the company.

On the back of the name card, I added a few ways people who are interested in the company can contact me by.

These incldue email, phone, and a website link.

I also added my name and my position in the company for informational purposes.

The entire project was done on Adobe Illustrator and I am extremely proud of how it turned out.
